Act as a senior motion designer and front-end developer.

Create a minimal, premium, and highly subtle animated background for a website hero section.

IMPORTANT:
This is for a high-end agency website. The animation must feel clean, intelligent, and non-distracting.

--------------------------------------------------

🎯 GOAL

Add depth and visual richness to the background WITHOUT distracting from the main text.

The motion should feel:

- calm
- premium
- controlled
- almost invisible but noticeable

--------------------------------------------------

🎨 STYLE

- Light/off-white background (#F7F7F5 or similar)
- Use very soft gray tones only (no strong colors)
- Maintain minimal contrast

--------------------------------------------------

🧩 ELEMENTS TO INCLUDE

Use a combination of:

1. Soft halftone dot patterns  
   - very low opacity  
   - slightly blurred  
   - placed in corners or edges  

2. Blurred abstract shapes  
   - organic shapes (not sharp)  
   - very soft edges  
   - extremely low visibility  

3. Minimal fine lines or curves  
   - thin, subtle  
   - geometric or flow-based  

4. Floating micro particles (optional)  
   - very slow movement  
   - barely noticeable  

--------------------------------------------------

🎬 ANIMATION BEHAVIOR

- ultra slow motion (almost static feel)
- smooth easing (no sharp transitions)
- subtle parallax effect on scroll
- gentle floating / drifting motion

Examples:
- halftone pattern slowly shifting position
- blurred shapes moving slightly over time
- particles drifting slowly

--------------------------------------------------

⚠️ STRICT RULES

- NO fast animation  
- NO bright colors  
- NO heavy gradients  
- NO distracting motion  
- NO sharp edges  
- NO visual noise  

Everything should feel:

👉 calm  
👉 controlled  
👉 premium  

--------------------------------------------------

📐 PLACEMENT

- keep center area clean (for text)
- motion elements mostly on edges / corners
- ensure readability is NOT affected

--------------------------------------------------

💡 PERFORMANCE

- lightweight animation
- optimized for fast loading
- responsive across devices

--------------------------------------------------

🎯 FINAL FEEL

The user should not consciously notice the animation.

But subconsciously feel:

👉 “this looks premium”
👉 “this feels alive”

--------------------------------------------------
