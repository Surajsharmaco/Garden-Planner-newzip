Here are the key SEO techniques to adopt:
Generative Engine Optimization (GEO): With SGE (Search Generative Experience) becoming standard, content should be tailored to be summarized by AI. Create content with clear titles, structured data, and concise, direct answers to questions to increase the likelihood of being quoted.
Topic Clusters & Content Hubs: Rather than focusing on individual keywords, build content hubs that cover a topic in depth to establish topical authority. This involves creating a pillar page and linking to multiple subtopic articles.
Targeting "People Also Ask" (PAA): Actively identify questions from the PAA section in Google search results and create content that specifically answers them, improving chances of appearing in featured snippets.
E-E-A-T Optimization: Ensure content showcases personal experience and expert knowledge. Use author bios, reputable citations, and create content in the voice of an authority to boost trustworthiness.
User Experience (UX) & Technical SEO: Prioritize mobile-first design, fast page loading speeds, and a secure (HTTPS) site, as these directly affect ranking. Focus on reducing bounce rates through high-quality, engaging content.
Leverage First-Party Data & User Behavior: As third-party cookies diminish, use your own website data to identify user intent and create targeted content that addresses specific audience behavior.
Unlinked Brand Mentions: Identify websites that mention your brand but do not link back to you. Reach out to these sites to convert those mentions into backlinks.
Optimize for Voice and Multimodal Search: As voice search rises, make sure your content is conversational, includes FAQ sections, and is easy for AI to read and articulate.
Visual SEO: Use descriptive alt text, optimize image sizes for speed, and create original images/infographics to improve engagement and visual search visibility.
Leverage AI Tools for Research: Utilize Gemini Deep Research to analyze competitors, identify keyword gaps, and map out content strategies to ensure your website meets the criteria for modern search engines.
