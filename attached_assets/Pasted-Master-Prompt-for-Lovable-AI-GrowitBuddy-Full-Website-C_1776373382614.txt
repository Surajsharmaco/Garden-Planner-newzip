Master Prompt for Lovable AI (GrowitBuddy Full Website)

Create a premium, modern, minimal, highly engaging website for a brand called GrowitBuddy.

The website must feel like a global authority agency that helps founders and creators build influence through content and distribution systems.

The design must be clean, professional, minimal, and easy to understand so visitors can quickly know what the company does.

Avoid confusing layouts.
Focus on clarity, hierarchy, and engagement.
